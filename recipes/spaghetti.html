Spaghetti with parmesan cheese

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Salt
  • Marinara sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot on the stove
  2. Add pasta noodles and salt
  3. Cover and cook for 10-12 minutes
  4. Drain pasta and put back in bowl
  5. Mix in sauce with pasta
  6. Serve and add parmesan chees to taste
